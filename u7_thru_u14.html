General Information

  • Levels: These correspond to a player's school grade in the Fall of a given year; U7 (“under 7”) is for 1st graders, U8 for 2nd graders, etc. up through U14 or 8th grade.
  • Uniforms: HWSC jerseys and other gear are available at Tursi Soccer (inside the Rose City Futsal building) on the East side, and The Far Post Soccer Supply on the West side.
  • Practice Schedules: Practice schedules are distributed by team coaches 1-2 weeks before the season's first practice is held.
  • More Information: Check out our Game Day page for information about PYSA soccer fields and game schedules.

Key Dates for U7 - U14 Spring Soccer

  • 1/9/19: Registration opens for all players and coaches
  • 3/1/19: Registration deadline for all players and coaches
  • 3/5/19: PYSA deadline for team registration
  • 4/6/19: First game of Spring season
  • 5/18/19: Last game of Spring season

Spring Season

  • Spring registration fees are $65 per player (uniform not included)
  • Spring season generally runs for 6-7 weeks starting in April, and most games are on Saturdays

Fall Season

  • For U7-U14, Fall registration fees are $100 per player (uniform not included)
  • Fall Season generally runs from the first week of school through the end of October or early November, and most games are on Saturdays
