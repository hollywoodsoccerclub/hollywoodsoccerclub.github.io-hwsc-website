2018 Summer Soccer Camp Options

Summer soccer camps can help kids improve their soccer skills in a fun and challenging environment. Several summer soccer camps are offered in Portland. The following are camps that we recommend based on positive past experiences of Hollywood Soccer Club players:


  • BaxterSports Soccer Camp: BaxterSports offers soccer camps for boys and girls. BaxterSports has recently hired more female coaches and is offering group discounts for girls! Check out this link for more details.

  • Rose City Futsal: Rose City Futsal's summer camps will teach your player ball control skills and fun games with professional club instructors. The facility is indoors and air conditioned, so the weather is always fine. Many HWSC players and coaches have played with RCF and its Youth Academy program.


    • Please note that Hollywood Soccer Club is not affiliated with these organizations, and we recommend that parents research the various options thoroughly before choosing a camp for their player.


    Spring & Fall Kinderkick

    Our Spring and Fall Kinderkick programs offer three 1-hour midweek sessions. Kinderkick is only open to players currently enrolled in Kindergarten and is not intended for younger Pre-K enthusiasts. The Spring Kinderkick program gives preference to returning Kinderkick players - please see the FAQ for more details.


    Spring & Fall Recreational Soccer

    HWSC registers players between the ages of 6 - 14, or first through eighth grades, for recreational soccer. Recreational soccer players can be new to the sport, or have prior experience. Teams are created based on interest and availability of volunteer coaches.
