Registration for Players and Coaches

Spring 2019 soccer registration opens on January 9th! Click here to register.


  • Subscribe to the club email list for notifications about registration opening dates and other important club announcements.
  • Like our Facebook page to stay informed about all things Hollywood Soccer Club.

Registration is handled on a first-come basis. Sign up early to reserve a space for your player!


  • U7-U14: $65 per player. U7-U14 program information is here.
  • Kinderkick: $45 per player. Kinderkick program information is here.
  • Coaches: We welcome new coaches!
  • Financial Assistance: Our club offers scholarships. Please contact our Registrar prior to registering your player if you seek financial assistance.
  • Scholarship Fund: If you'd like to support our club scholarship fund, please donate during registration checkout.


All families are encouraged to review our Team Formation Policy and seek answers to any soccer-related questions in the FAQ. Still have questions? Please contact the Registrar or any other club Officer.


Thank you for your continued support of Hollywood Soccer Club.







* Fees subject to change without notification
