Timbers & Thorns Summer Camps at Grant Park

There are still spots available for kids aged 5 through 13 at the Timbers Hollywood Soccer Camp the week of July 14 through July 18 and the Thorns Hollywood Soccer Camp the week of July 28 through August 1 at Grant Park.


For more information and to register, please visit the Portland Timbers & Thorns FC 2014 Camps page.

Fall 2014 Cleat Exchange - Info Coming Soon!

What: Opportunity for you to exchange your clean and gently-worn cleats with other members of our soccer community. You do not need to trade in a pair in order to take home a 'new' pair. We are also accepting for exchange any clean used club jerseys and shin guards your player may have outgrown.


When: TBA, Early September


Where: Near the Grant HS flagpole.


Details: You may drop off your clean and lightly worn soccer gear beforehand (preferred method) on the porch of the McKerlick home located at 3584 NE US Grant Place (SW corner of US Grant Pl and 36th) or you may bring them same-day to the event.


Questions?: contact Ted Batt (ttbatt@yahoo.com)
