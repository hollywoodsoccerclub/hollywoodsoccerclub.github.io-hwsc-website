Jerseys at Far Post's Eastside Location

Need a new Hollywood Soccer Club jersey? Lucky us! Far Post Soccer Supply, our jersey vendor, now has a store in our neighborhood, located inside Rose City Futsal.


All players U7-U14 need a jersey, and they’re the same as before (red with white collar and our logo on the top left). So, if you’re in need of a jersey the next size up, or if your U7 player is ready to take the field this fall with our club, stop by Far Post at Rose City Futsal and purchase one.
