SafeSport Training

Beginning in Fall 2019, each team administrator (head coach, assistant coach, and team manager) and club administrator / board member is required to complete SafeSport Training. There are three training modules, and you'll receive an all-inclusive certificate from SafeSport when all three are completed. Please note that certification is done annually. The initial year includes the full training, the following year a short refresher training, then the full training, and so on.


The SafeSport coursework covers the following topics in-depth:

  • Mandatory Reporting
  • Sexual Misconduct Awareness Education
  • Emotional and Physical Misconduct

The initial training is about 90 minutes but you can stop and come back to it without losing your place. Click here to more information about SafeSport and how to take the courses.
