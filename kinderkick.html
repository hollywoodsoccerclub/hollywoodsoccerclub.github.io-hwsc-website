General Information

  • Kinderkick is Hollywood Soccer Club's special soccer program for children currently enrolled in Kindergarten.
  • The program includes fun activities and informal scrimmages with teammates.
  • Kinderkick is offered in the Spring and Fall.
  • Registration includes a team jersey.
  • The one-hour per week program meets for multiple consecutive weeks each season.
  • The program focuses on skill development, ball touches and small-sided games.
  • Contact our Kinderkick Coordinator for more information.

Spring Season

  • Cost: Spring 2020 fees are $45 per player, plus an additional $1 transaction fee.
  • Also: The program gives priority to returning Kinderkick players.
  • More Spring 2020 Kinderkick details are coming soon!

Fall Season

  • Cost: Fall 2019 registration fees are $55 per player.
  • When: Fall Kinderkick will be held on six consecutive Thursdays from 5 - 6 pm. The dates are: September 12, September 19, September 26, October 3, October 10 and October 17.
  • Where: Beverly Cleary Fernwood Campus, 1915 NE 33rd Ave, Portland, OR 97212
  • Also: Fall Kinderkick is only open to players who will be enrolled in Kindergarten during the 2019 - 2020 school year and is not intended for younger Pre-K enthusiasts.
