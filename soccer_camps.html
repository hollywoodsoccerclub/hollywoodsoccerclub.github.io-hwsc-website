Summer Soccer Camps

Summer soccer camps can help kids improve their soccer skills in a fun and challenging environment. Several summer soccer camps are offered in Portland. The following are camps that we recommend based on positive past experiences of Hollywood Soccer Club players:


  • BaxterSports Soccer Camp: BaxterSports offers soccer camps as well as all-sports camps for boys and girls. Their camps are held outdoors, in Portland parks. More information about BaxterSports camps for summer 2019 is available here.

  • Rose City Futsal: Rose City Futsal's summer camps teach ball control skills and fun games with professional club instructors, in an air-conditioned, indoor facility. Many HWSC players and coaches have played with RCF and its Youth Academy program.

Please note that Hollywood Soccer Club is not affiliated with these organizations, and we recommend that parents research the various options thoroughly before choosing a camp for their player.
